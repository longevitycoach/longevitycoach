Optimal Reference Values

Based on the work of Dr. Ulrich Strunz and Dr. med. Helena Orfanos-Boeckel, these are the target ranges I'm using for optimal health.

Vitamins & Minerals

Vitamin D 40-60 ng/mL
Vitamin B12 > 600 pg/mL
Ferritin 100-200 ng/mL

Hormones

TSH 0.5-2.0 µIU/mL
Free T3 3.0-4.5 pg/mL
Free T4 1.2-1.8 ng/dL

Lipids

LDL Cholesterol < 100 mg/dL
